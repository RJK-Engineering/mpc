# Shortcut Keys

Key     | Action | Reply
------- | ------ | -----
h ?     | Help
s       | Status
l       | List
r       | Reset
p       | Pause monitoring
q Esc   | Quit
**Mode**
a       | AutoCompleteMode
b       | BookmarkMode
O       | OpenMode
**Per category**
c       | SetCategory
C       | CompleteCategory
**Current snapshot**
o       | Open
t       | Tag
u       | Undo
**All**
d       | Delete
m       | Move
